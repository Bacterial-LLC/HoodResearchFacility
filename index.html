Our Team

Recursionn

Recursionn is a dedicated DevSecOps Engineer with over 4 years of experience in integrating security practices within the DevOps process. With a strong background in software development, system administration, and security protocols, Recursionn excels at fostering collaboration between development, operations, and security teams to ensure the delivery of secure and high-quality software.

Recursionn has a proven track record of implementing automated security testing and continuous integration/continuous deployment (CI/CD) pipelines, utilizing tools such as Jenkins, GitLab CI, and Docker. Skilled in cloud security, particularly with platforms like AWS and Azure, and adept at using infrastructure as code (IaC) tools such as Terraform and Ansible to enhance security posture.

Passionate about staying ahead of emerging threats, Recursionn actively participates in security training and workshops, and holds certifications such as Certified Kubernetes Security Specialist (CKS) and AWS Certified Security – Specialty. Committed to promoting a culture of security awareness within teams and ensuring compliance with industry standards and regulations.

In their free time, Recursionn enjoys contributing to open-source projects, participating in hackathons, and sharing knowledge through blogging and speaking engagements.
